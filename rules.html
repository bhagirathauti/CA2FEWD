Rules to play Archery 3D

1.The maximum points the player can get are 8 , and minimum are 0
2.If the player hits in the centre , score will be +8 .
3.If player hits on the edges he would get score ,according to the distance of edge from centre.
4.Player will get 10 moves initially.
5.If player gets 8 score, 3 extra moves will be given and for 7 score 2 extra moves.
6.A timer is present in a game,player gets a specified time to shoot,if timer is finished move will be auto-reduced.
7.When the moves are finished game is over.
